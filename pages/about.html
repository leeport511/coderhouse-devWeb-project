AXIOS GAMES

WE ARE

We are a passionate indie video game studio focus on creating new and amazing interactive experiences for the entire world.

Axios Games foundational pillars rely on five core values that, per our vision, every creative entrepreneurship should have.

Pasion, our principal and daily fuel that drives us to build not only great games but also a great company.
Candor, we strongly believe that frankness is one of the most important pillars of any creative entrepreneurship.
Open Mind, it is crucial to our work to always stay open to new ideas and new ways of working.
Constructive Criticism, we aim to collaboratively improve the overall quality of our products taking criticism impersonal through candor and doing it in a positive way.
And Self Discipline, as the expression of inner strength and staying power to overcome any mistakes.
